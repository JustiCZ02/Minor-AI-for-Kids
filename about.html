AI Data Camp

About the Project

A student-built guide to AI literacy for ages 12-15.

AI Data Camp helps teens understand how AI shows up in their daily apps, how data trains models, and how to stay safe with privacy. The experience includes short learning modules, an AI Mentor chat, and hands-on practice spotting deepfakes.

Start learning Teacher Toolkit

Who it is for

  • Secondary school students (12-15)
  • Teachers who want ready-made AI lessons
  • Parents and families exploring AI together

What students do

  • Learn how AI uses data and patterns
  • Discuss privacy, fairness, and trust
  • Practice spotting deepfake clues
  • Debate good and risky AI uses

Skills they build

  • Critical thinking and media literacy
  • Safer data habits and permission choices
  • Clear questions and healthy skepticism
  • Team discussion and reflection

Mix the activities to match your time and class level.

  • Run the AI Mentor chat as a whole-class discussion.
  • Finish with the deepfake practice to build smart skepticism.
