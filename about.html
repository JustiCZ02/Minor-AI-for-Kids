About AI Data Camp

A student-built prototype to grow AI literacy and digital citizenship.

This space is designed for teens 12-15 years old to explore how AI works, where it shows up in their daily apps, and how to stay safe with data. We pair short lessons with quizzes and challenges so learning stays hands-on.

Start learning

Who is this for?

AI Data Camp is built for classrooms, families, and curious students.

  • Secondary school students (12-15)
  • Teachers and schools
  • Parents who want to talk about AI with their children

What you can learn

Core topics covered across the modules, quizzes, and challenges.

  • What AI is and where you see it
  • How data trains AI models
  • Why privacy matters
  • Ways to stay safe and think critically
  • How AI shapes feeds, filters, and games
