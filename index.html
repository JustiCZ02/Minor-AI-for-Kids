AI Data Camp

Welcome to your friendly AI hub for students aged 12 & 15.

Here you learn what AI is, how it trains on data, where it shows up in your apps, and how to stay smart and safe while using it. We explain it with simple words and examples from games, social media, and school life.

Jump into our guided step-by-step module to see how AI works under the hood, then come back for quizzes, challenges, and teacher tools you can share in class. Explore at your own pace and remember: you are in control of your data and your choices.
